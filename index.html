About Wabash Systems

25 Years in the Trenches. Now Bringing It Home.

My name is Andy Gray. I've spent 25 years running e-commerce and IT operations for a national retailer in Indianapolis — not advising from the sidelines, but actually doing it. Managing a $700–800K/month online store. Leading a solo platform migration from Magento to BigCommerce. Running SEO since before most small businesses had a website. Handling LTL freight logistics, ERP integrations, Google Ads, call center operations, and everything in between.

Flooring is one of the most complex products you can sell online — $2,000 shipping quotes, pallet deliveries, hundreds of variants, custom calculators. If I could build and run that operation profitably for two decades, I can help you sell just about anything.

I'm coming home to the Wabash Valley. My family is here, and I'm bringing everything I learned in Indianapolis with me. Wabash Systems exists because the businesses here deserve the same level of digital expertise that big-city retailers have had for years — and they've been going without it for too long.

When you work with Wabash Systems, you're working directly with me. No account managers, no junior associates, no outsourced work. Just 25 years of hard-won experience pointed at your business.
