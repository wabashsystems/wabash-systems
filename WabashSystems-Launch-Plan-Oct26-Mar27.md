# WabashSystems — 6-Month Launch Plan
*October 2026 – March 2027*
*Last updated: April 2026*

---

## The Goal

Hit **3 retainer clients at $1,000–1,500/month** before the move to Hutsonville. That's $3,000–4,500 MRR — enough to cover basics while building the local client base post-move. Two clients is survivable. One is uncomfortable. Zero means you moved too soon.

---

## October 2026 — Launch Outreach

This is go time on client acquisition.

- **LinkedIn goes live this month** — needed before outreach lands credibly
- Identify 20–30 Indianapolis targets with weak or no online stores
- Send first wave of outreach emails — offer free audits to start conversations
- File the **Illinois Series LLC** — do it now before move chaos starts
- **Goal:** 3–5 active conversations, 1 signed client by end of month

---

## November 2026 — Convert and Build

- Follow up on October outreach — don't let conversations go cold
- Convert at least one audit to a retainer
- Start delivering work — figure out your service delivery workflow, reporting structure, tooling
- Finalize **MSA and retainer agreement** if not already done
- **Goal:** 1–2 paying clients, first invoice sent

---

## December 2026 — Consolidate, Don't Chase

Holidays slow everything on the client side — don't fight it.

- Focus on delivering great work for existing clients
- Refine your process and systems
- Get **GL + E&O insurance** quotes finalized and policies bound
- Open **business checking account** if not already done
- Set aside January 15 estimated tax payment now — don't get caught short
- **Goal:** Solid delivery, systems dialed in, finances clean

---

## January 2027 — Second Wave Outreach + Tax Prep

- **Estimated taxes due January 15** — don't miss it
- New year = new budgets for small businesses — reactivate December conversations
- Start targeting **southeastern Illinois businesses remotely** — warm those relationships before you arrive
- Second outreach wave to Indianapolis targets who didn't respond in October/November
- **Goal:** 2–3 active retainer clients

---

## February 2027 — Pre-Move Ramp

- **Stop taking large one-time projects** — anything not wrapped before the move is a liability
- Focus on retainer clients only — they travel with you
- Get **Google Ads certified** if not already done (free via Skillshop)
- Begin logistical move prep
- **Goal:** $3,000–5,000 MRR locked in before leaving Indianapolis

---

## March 2027 — Move Month

- Keep retainer client work running without interruption
- Communicate proactively: "We're relocating our base of operations to Illinois — no interruption to your service."
- Set up **Google Business Profile** for new Hutsonville-area location once address is confirmed
- Hit the **Crawford County Chamber of Commerce** immediately after arrival
- **Goal:** Seamless transition, clients retained, local presence established

## Outreach Strategy (Indianapolis, Pre-Move)

1. Identify 20–30 small businesses with weak or no online presence
2. Send short, specific email — reference their specific gap, offer a free audit
3. Follow up once after 5–7 days if no response
4. Convert audit to retainer — audit should surface enough problems to make the next step obvious
5. Signed MSA + SOW before any work begins

**Target verticals for Indianapolis outreach:**
- Specialty retailers with physical stores and no online channel
- Home goods and boutique shops
- Local service businesses selling physical products
- Restaurants with catering or direct-to-consumer potential
